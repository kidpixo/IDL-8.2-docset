Window Routines

IDLgrWindow - Represents an on-screen area on a display device that serves as a graphics destination.

WDELETE Procedure - Deletes IDL graphics windows.

WIDGET_WINDOW - Creates a graphics widget.

WINDOW - Creates an empty graphics window.

WINDOW Procedure - Creates a window for the display of graphics or text. (Direct Graphics)

WSET - Selects the current window. (Direct Graphics)

WSHOW - Exposes or hides the designated window. (Direct Graphics)
